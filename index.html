Meet Jeremy Bartels
Founding Partner

⚖️
Jeremy Bartels Founding Partner

Jeremy Bartels is the founder of The Bartels Law Firm, where he represents clients in a variety of civil litigation contexts including employment law, insurance law, personal injury and other complex litigation matters. With over 17 years of legal practice, Jeremy provides sophisticated legal representation with the personal attention every client deserves.

Jeremy is also honored to frequently serve as a Judge Pro Tempore for several Western Washington jurisdictions, entrusted by judges to conduct court and perform judicial duties. His distinguished career includes four years as a Deputy Prosecuting Attorney for Snohomish County, where he achieved a 98% conviction rate across dozens of trials, and time with the Washington State Attorney General's Office, where he never lost a jury trial.

Jeremy graduated cum laude from Seattle University School of Law, where he was twice the mock trial champion and served on the distinguished Moot Court Board and Order of the Barristers. He earned his Bachelor's Degree in Biomedical Engineering from Vanderbilt University. His diverse background includes arguing the seminal case Davis v. Baugh before the Washington Supreme Court, which resulted in the permanent excision of a legal standard that had been in place for 100 years.

Personal Injury

Helping You Recover and Move Forward

When you've been injured due to someone else's negligence, you need an attorney who will pursue fair compensation for your losses. We have successfully represented clients in personal injury matters, recovering significant settlements and verdicts.

Being injured is not only painful—it can impact every aspect of your life. Medical expenses, lost wages, and the inability to work can create significant financial pressure. We work diligently to pursue the compensation you need to move forward.

⚖️ Experienced representation for serious injury cases

SIGNATURE PRACTICE

Insurance Coverage Disputes

Protecting Your Rights as a Policyholder

You pay premiums with the expectation that your insurance company will honor its obligations when you need coverage. When insurers wrongfully deny valid claims or engage in bad faith practices, we are here to help you pursue the benefits you're entitled to receive.

The Bartels Law Firm has extensive experience litigating insurance coverage disputes across all types of policies, including automobile, homeowners, business, health, and disability insurance. We understand insurance law and how to hold carriers accountable.

⚖️ Experienced advocacy for policyholders facing coverage denials
