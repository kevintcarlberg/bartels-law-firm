Personal Injury

Getting Your Life Back

The Bartels Law Firm has a proven track record of obtaining just and favorable results for people who have been injured as a result of the negligence of others. Being injured is not only painful, it can take over your life.

Injuries can stop you from doing all of the activities you enjoy, rob you of your time with your loved ones, and make you feel like you will never be the same person again. The burdens are not only emotional—medical expenses and time lost at work can heap tremendous pressure on top of an already frustrating situation.

Trust our firm with your case and we will do everything we can to alleviate these burdens by obtaining a favorable financial outcome for you as quickly as possible.

Insurance Litigation

Proven Results

Nearly everyone has some sort of insurance. Not everyone is treated fairly by their insurance company. The law protects those of us that purchase insurance from unfair treatment.

The Bartels Law Firm has litigated insurance coverage matters on behalf of insureds for all types of coverage, from automobile insurance to business policies to healthcare coverage. We have a proven track record of helping our clients obtain the benefits they deserve and hold their insurance companies accountable for denying coverage.

If you believe you have been unfairly denied coverage or other benefits by your insurance company, let The Bartels Law Firm be the solution for your legal needs.
